## Contributing

PRs welcome — especially more ASCII species, additional chiptune SFX variants, and UX polish on the info pages. For bigger changes (wire protocol, new BLE commands), file an issue first or open one on [upstream](https://github.com/anthropics/claude-desktop-buddy/issues).

## ⭐ Show support

If you're running this on your own Cardputer, a star helps other makers find the fork. If you want to share a photo of it on your desk, open a discussion or tag me.
